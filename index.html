Hi! I am a PhD candidate at Zhejiang University, College of Education (graduating in June 2026). I am supervised by Yan Li and co-supervised by Fei Wu, Wenzhi Chen, and Xuesong Zhai.

I was a visiting PhD student at Oxford University, Department of Computer Science, Human-Centered Computing Group, supervised by Jun Zhao during 2022-2023.

My research focuses on the intersection of Educational Technology (EdTech), human-computer interaction (HCI), and AI Curriculum Design, especially for early adolescents' formal learning.

I am passionate about empowering children, families, and teachers in EdTech to protect their digital autonomy. I have a stats background, am familiar with mixed-methods analysis, and am learning to fine-tune LLM toolkits such as LangChain.
